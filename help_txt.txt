You play Cribbage against the computer.
The computer has a simple AI to calculate it's 
best hand and cards to play in the lay part of
the game. The computer bases no decisions on cards held
in your hand, i.e an honest game.
To start add new player from the menu.
You can have any number of users/players.
Each players stats are recorded and can 
viewed from the stats overview menu
or in graph form from the graph
menu.
Players can be deleted from the menu.
Q to quit during game play, stats are not recorded 
unless the game is played to the end.
